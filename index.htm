Why Mercer County Park and not the old place?
Music. We're looking to host the Lonesome Pines, a stellar Americana style roots band from NJ. You may recognize one of the members. Amplified music is not permitted at Thompson Park. Also, Mercer County Park is arguably the best park in NJ, centrally located, and very close to public transportation. Check it out.

Located alongside Lake Mercer and within walking distance of the Marina, is the Mercer Park West Picnic Area. The area includes a 30-foot by 60-foot pavilion and is equipped with 1 large and 1 small charcoal cooking grill, electricity, and running water, 14 picnic tables under the pavilion and scattered throughout picnic area, and benches overlooking Lake Mercer. Paddleboats are available for rent at the lake.
